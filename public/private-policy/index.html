Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your data.

Overview

TopMoneyTools ("we," "us," "our," or "the service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to your use of our website and calculators.

What Data We Don't Collect

We do not store any calculation data or personal information. All calculations performed in our tools are done locally in your browser. We do not:

  • Store your calculation inputs or results
  • Collect personal information like names or email addresses
  • Use cookies to track your activity
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use analytics tracking (like Google Analytics)

What Limited Information We May Receive

Our web hosting provider (Google Cloud Run) may automatically collect technical information such as:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website

This information is collected in standard server logs and is used only for:

  • Website performance and optimization
  • Security and abuse prevention
  • Understanding general usage patterns

How We Use Information

We only use technical information for:

  • Maintaining and improving our service
  • Protecting against fraud and abuse
  • Complying with legal obligations

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to secure. However, we take the security of our service seriously:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Our hosting platform (Google Cloud Run) provides enterprise-grade security
  • We regularly update our software and dependencies

Local Storage

Our calculators do not use browser local storage, cookies, or any other persistent storage mechanisms to track your activity or preferences.

Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on Google Cloud Run. Google Cloud may collect technical information as described in their privacy notice.

We do not use third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is limited personal data for you to request or have removed. If you have concerns about our practices, please contact us.

Children's Privacy

TopMoneyTools is not directed at children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete such information promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us.
